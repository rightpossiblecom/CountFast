Short description:
Fast visual counting game that sharpens focus in quick sessions.

Full description:
CountFast is a fast-paced counting game designed for short, addictive sessions.

How it works:
1. Objects appear on screen for a moment.
2. They disappear.
3. You pick how many you saw.

Game modes:
- Classic Mode: 3 lives, increasing difficulty each round.
- Time Attack: 60-second sprint for your highest score.

Why players love CountFast:
- Fast rounds with immediate feedback
- Smooth transitions and clean visuals
- High score and streak tracking
- Daily high score motivation
- Easy to learn, hard to master

CountFast is built for quick breaks, focus training, and replayable challenge. Play one round and try to beat your best score.

Privacy:
CountFast does not collect personal data. Scores and settings are stored locally on your device.
