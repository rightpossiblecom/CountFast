# CountFast Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

CountFast does not collect, store, or share personal data.

## Data Collection
- We do not collect personal information such as names, emails, phone numbers, or location.
- We do not use analytics or tracking services.
- We do not require account creation or login.

## Local Storage
- The app stores gameplay data locally on your device (for example: high score, streak, and settings).
- This data never leaves your device.

## Data Sharing
- We do not sell, transfer, or share user data with third parties.

## Children
- CountFast does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Contact
For support questions, contact: support@countfast.app
